PSA Platforms

Track time, schedule resources, produce billing runs. The best ones can surface project risks and reduce unbilled time. But they start from one assumption: that what was logged is true.

Their ceiling

They know what was logged. Not whether it was true, contextually relevant, or executed by the optimal person for that task.

Track applications, keystrokes, screen activity. An employee using a spreadsheet application is equally “active” whether building a client deliverable or a personal budget.
